We've curated guidelines for our community to use in order to create, maintain and expand the jQuery brand.

Introduction

These guidelines are the road map for creating, maintaining and using the various brands within the family of jQuery projects. The guidelines cover logos, colors, typography, and naming conventions to help you understand the rules of our brand.

By adhering to these guidelines, you'll play a part in making jQuery and its family of brands recognizable, memorable and as strong as possible!
