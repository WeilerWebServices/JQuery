Nuts & Bolts

The 2012 jQuery Developer Summit was held at the Aol Campus in Dulles, Virginia, just outside Washington, DC, on Monday, October 15th and Tuesday, October 16th.

All Hands On Deck!

Join the entire jQuery team for a deep dive into the project and the world of open source development. Get the lay of the land, and learn how you can get involved, making changes that will benefit the entire community.

We're looking for people who can write code, implement a design, create documentation, make test cases to triage bugs, or people who can solve those bugs! Oh, and people who want to have fun while learning a lot (and staying well-fed).

You don't need to be an expert in every aspect of development, design, jQuery, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and so on. If you can bring expertise in just one of these areas and can collaborate effectively, you'll have a great time and learn a lot from your peers!
